HZDR Excursion

The Technische Universität Dresden closely collaborates with the Helmholtz-Center Dresden-Rossendorf (HZDR), the nearby facility known for its large scale experiments. As a DyProSo-2023 attendee, you will have the unique opportunity to visit two such large experiments at HZDR, namely the “Free-Electron Laser” Laboratory (FELBE) and the “High-Magnetic-Field” Laboratory (HLD). This visit is scheduled for Friday September 29, 2023, right after the DyProSo-2023 conference. Booking is required for participation of the day, which can be done during online registration.
